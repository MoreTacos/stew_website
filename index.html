About

Stew Arevalo

Since 1995, Stew has been teaching volleyball players from ages 10 to 18 at an elite level nationally and internationally, using techniques taught all over the world.

Athletes selected to be in the Elite training program by Stew have the option of practicing up to three times a week for the entire summer (Mid May-End August). Training sessions will be led personally by Stew.

Players with exceptional skill may be invited by Stew to play on organized tournaments every Saturday to play against the best in Ontario.
